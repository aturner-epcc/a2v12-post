ARCHER2 is the UK National Supercomputing Service. The ARCHER2 Service is:

A world-class supercomputer located and run in the UK.
An invaluable resource for researchers who study problems with a global impact. Part of the PRACE initiative giving leading scientific users access to a European pool of supercomputers.
